    2021 Glitch Project
    Mediums: Notepad and Audacity
    Size: 2269 × 3410 pixels

    The series of my glitch artwork portrays the theme of nature with traditional Korean architecture with the interruption of the digital world. I used Audacity to create the distinctive textures and effects of glitches that you can not make on Notepad. The gradual disappearance of colors and distortion of the environment from Artwork 1 to Artwork 4 additionally symbolizes the memory of nature slowly getting lost. However, if the four pieces of work are put in the opposite order, it would give the opposite interpretation. The continuous recurrence of colors and clarity of the environment would be seen as if the memory is coming back together and the scenes of nature are puzzled up.
