    In June 2021, I began working on a project to create plush dolls of my favorite KPOP boyband, Tomorrow X Together. After six months of work, I was finally able to accomplish this goal in December of the same year. The dolls were sold my own online shop, where I sell a variety of products featuring characters inspired by KPOP, including art prints, keychains, enamel pins, and stickers.

    As an artist and a fan of this group, I poured my heart into designing each animal character to closely resemble the facial characteristics and personality traits of the corresponding member. It brings me immense joy to share my passion with others who appreciate my art and support my work.
