    The idea of this piece explores the intense bond between companion animals and humans. I used the strong light source from the top and front to add a sense of the person's strong longing emotion towards the animal parrot. In contrast to the living person's shadows, I used a saturated pinkish orange as the piece's focus color to represent the memory of the parrot, which is as hazy as shadows.
