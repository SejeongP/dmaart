    I utilized the Parametric Equations created by p5 and incorporated my own methods to introduce a three-dimensional element to the animation. By implementing the bounce effect, I was able to move the lines around the canvas in a dynamic motion. In addition, I brought the framecount function make the colors change at specific frames, including white, purple, and randomized colors. I consider the 3D aspect of the animation to be a successful part in this project. Aside from varying the sets of lines and colors, I believe that additional functions could be integrated to diversify the animation's consistent pattern.
