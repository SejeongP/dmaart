    Sejeong Park is a second-year student at San Jose State University, majoring in Digital Media Art. Throughout her childhood and teenage life, art has consistently been a part of her life, and she aspires to become a digital media artist who shares engaging, creative, and aesthetic work in the future. As a Digital Media Art major, Sejeong has been exploring various mediums and creating her own works. In particular, she is fascinated by the interactive and engaging projects that showcase how art can connect people beyond the barriers of language, culture, and background.

    As a designer and growing artist, Sejeong is seeking to find something that she finds truly enjoyable and wants to pursue as a career. So far, she has experimented from creative coding to building a website, creating designs, video filming, 3D sculpting, and photo editing. Despite the frustration that comes with working with a new program or technology for the first time, Sejeong finds it rewarding once she surpasses a certain threshold and is able to freely explore and create in the digital world. Her work in the digital environment has inspired her to continue searching for a driving force as an artist and share that joy with others through her creations.
